Why homeowners choose rolldowns

Why this is a different kind of project than standard enclosure work

A rolldown system is not quoted like a fixed screen wall. Opening size, system count, and wind exposure all change the setup.

Open when you want it open

The system retracts when you want the space open and deploys when you want coverage.

Privacy, shade, and added protection

Rolldowns are often chosen for privacy, sun control, and better control over exposed openings.

Quoted by opening size

Opening width, opening height, system count, and exposure conditions all affect the final price.

FAQ

Rolldown questions homeowners usually ask first

Should I start with the online estimate for rolldowns?

If you know the rough opening sizes and how many systems you want, yes. The online estimate is the quickest way to get a ballpark number before the site visit.

Do wind conditions matter?

Yes. Homes near the beach or in higher-wind conditions may need a different setup.

Can rolldowns be part of a larger enclosure project?

Yes. Some projects combine rolldowns with composite roof or other enclosure work depending on how the space is being used.
