About Our Company

Enclosures done
The Right Way

We build and repair pool enclosures, lanais, and screen rooms throughout North Florida with a focus on long-term durability and honest workmanship. Every project is personally overseen by the owner—from the initial quote to the final screw. We take pride in using quality materials, keeping clean worksites, and delivering consistent results. Our goal isn’t to be the biggest - it’s to be the most reliable. That means showing up on time, doing the job right, and making sure you're fully satisfied before we pack up.

Hands-On Expertise

You’re not dealing with a sales team - you’re working directly with the person who measures, plans, and builds your enclosure. That means no miscommunication, no surprises, and full accountability every step of the way.

Respect for Your Space

We keep the work area tidy and organized throughout the project. Materials and tools are handled with care, and we clean up thoroughly at the end of each day to minimize disruption to your home.

Why choose us?

We are fully licensed and insured (DBPR License #SCC131153585), giving you peace of mind that all work meets state requirements and is backed by full liability coverage.

We prioritize clear, timely communication. From the first call to final walkthrough, you’ll speak directly with the person building your enclosure - no middlemen, no delays.

All labor and parts are backed by a one-year guarantee. If any issues come up, we handle them directly - no runaround, no excuses.

The owner personally completes your installation—not rushed subcontractors. We focus on doing it right the first time, with attention to detail and craftsmanship that speaks for itself.

Our Services

New Enclosure Construction

We design and build screen enclosures from the ground up—engineered for durability, tailored to your layout, and built using only high-quality, American-made materials.

Contact Us

Concrete & Paver Work

No need to coordinate with outside crews - we handle the concrete slabs and paver bases for your enclosure, so your project runs smoother from start to finish.

Contact Us

Rebuild & Replacement

Whether storm-damaged or worn out, we replace aging enclosures with structurally sound, visually clean builds that restore your outdoor space - often better than the original.

Contact Us

Composite Roof Enclosures

Motorized Screens

Rescreening
