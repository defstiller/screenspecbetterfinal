Planning support

The parts around the enclosure still affect the job

Even when the enclosure is the main focus, the surrounding conditions still change how the job gets priced and built.

Footing, slab, and paver conditions

Existing slab, pavers, or missing footing can change how the enclosure gets planned. The estimate tool accounts for those conditions so the number is closer to the real job.

Existing roof tie-ins

Some projects are not free-standing enclosures. They are screen walls added under an existing roof, which changes the wall count, measurement path, and quoting logic.

Motorized opening protection

Rolldowns do not quote the same way a standard enclosure does. Opening count, opening size, and wind exposure all affect the final setup.
