Are your screen enclosure cables ready for the next storm?

Screen Enclosure Maintenance
June 23, 2024

For those of us who value the safety and longevity of our homes, regular inspections of your screen enclosure cables are essential. Here’s why you should make it a priority:

Properly maintained cables can save you thousands of dollars down the road.

Enhance Stability:

Tie-downs or cables are vital for stability, reducing lateral movement by approximately 60%. This added stability ensures your enclosure can withstand high winds, protecting your home and outdoor living space from screen tears and structural damage.

Minimize Stress and Prevent Damage:

Properly installed cables lessen the load on your enclosure by about 28%, which is especially significant in high-wind regions like Florida. This stress reduction helps prevent structural damage and extends the lifespan of your screen enclosure.

Peace of Mind:

With the investment you've made in your home, maintaining its value and safety is paramount. Ensure your screen enclosure is ready for any weather conditions.

It doesn't take long to test your cables, and it is always worth it.
